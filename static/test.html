About RoeRide

About RoeRide

RoeRide is a ride-sharing platform designed to facilitate transportation among students of Roehampton University. Our goal is to provide an affordable, sustainable, and convenient way for students to commute to and from campus. By connecting users traveling in the same direction, RoeRide helps students save money, reduce traffic congestion, and lower their carbon footprint.

We understand that commuting can be a hassle, especially for students. That's why we offer a seamless experience from finding a ride to reaching your destination. Our platform ensures safety and reliability with user ratings and reviews, making your journey stress-free.

Join us in making commuting easier, cheaper, and greener for everyone at Roehampton University.

FAQs

What is RoeRide?

RoeRide is a ride-sharing platform designed to facilitate transportation among students of Roehampton University.

How can I join RoeRide?

To get started, simply sign up on our platform and start browsing available rides.

Is RoeRide safe?

Yes, RoeRide ensures safety with user ratings and reviews, making your journey secure and reliable.

How much does it cost?

RoeRide is affordable, with costs shared among passengers, making it cheaper than traditional commuting options.

Can I list my ride?

Yes, you can list your ride and offer seats to other students traveling in the same direction.

What if I need to cancel a ride?

You can cancel a ride at any time, but please inform your passengers as soon as possible.
